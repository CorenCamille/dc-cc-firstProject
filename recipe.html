Homemade Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 3lb Chunck of Beef, 1 1/5 lb of potatoes and carrots, 1 box of hearty beef broth

Steps to make Beef Stew

  1. Cut Up Beef
  2. Prep Steamed Vegetables
  3. Season Meat
  4. Heat Stovetop on medium/high
  5. Cook 3lb of meat for 20-25 min.
  6. Add precooked steamed vegetables into saucepan
